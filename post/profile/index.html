Profile

January 1, 2018
profile

I created this blog to journal my progress towards becoming a data scientist an applied statistician. While my interest was initially piqued by the data science hype train, I feel that my interest in this discipline is probably more closely aligned with the traditional school of applied statistics, rather than the newer “hacker-culture” school of data science. I am as interested (if not more) in model interpretability and the “why” behind the answers as much as I am in building accurate models.

One major appeal of working in statistics / data science is that the skills gained are largely industry-agnostic and presents me with an opportunity to work in a variety of fields. Ultimately, I hope to be able to use modern statistical techniques and technologies to make a positive contribution to society in one or more of the following areas:

  • education and learning analytics;
  • mental health;
  • aged care and the elderly;
  • minorities and equality;
  • crime and justice.

Goals for 2018

Here are some of the things I hope to achieve this year:
