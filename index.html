Doing Data Science (Book)
Chapter 1 - Introduction: What Is Data Science? With years of experience in both academia and industry, authors Cathy O’Neil & Rachel Schutt look beyond the hype to explore what the data science discipline entails and looks like in practice. They also explore an idea that is often overlooked when speaking about data science - that building models and working with data is not value-neutral.
